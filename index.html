Debate

Debate Participation promotes problem-solving and innovative thinking, and helps students to build links between words and ideas that make concepts more meaningful and in practice to develop the art of public speaking and rhetoric.

Spelling Bee

The benefits of spelling bees extend beyond language: Since children are required to spell words while on stage, kids also develop self-confidence, communication and public speaking skills, and the ability to thrive under pressure..
